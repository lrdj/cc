# Howl Contest Entry

Do you remember River Rat? Dancing in the dregs,

Do you recall Phi Epsilon? Do you remember kegs?

In Manhattan, at Columbia, there was a microcosm of

America,

And the word up there was the word everywhere:

Culture war no more.
